Computer Vision

I took a computer vision course COSC428 at the University of Canterbury in my final year of engineering which consisted of coming up with a project based on computer vision, developing the project into some sort of result, and writing an academic paper on the subject.

I chose detecting and identifying cyclists as they crossed the lap/finish line during a race which involved computer vision functions and a nueral network.

Through my research and application, I was lucky enough to publish my paper at the IVCNZ 2019 conference.
