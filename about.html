Introduction
I'm Marco, a Kiwi-Canadian Aerospace Engineer from New Zealand currently traveling the World, and as you could tell, space is a significant focus of my life.

I appreciate creativity and dedication when it comes to solving problems. My university didn't have a robotics club, so I founded one. A computer vision course I took presented an opportunity to write a paper on it, so I published it.
I've dedicated my passions for space towards my professional life and get to work on exciting projects such as supporting the testing and qualification of flight hardware and software of the Electron rocket, and rocket launch mission operations!

Otherwise, my passions lie in coding, robotics, cooking, photography, and exploring the world (until I get the chance to explore off-world).
